## Variables in the tidy_data.txt file
* subject - The id of the person or subject
* activity - The descriptive activity names: WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING and LAYING

The following are variables which contain measurement for the mean "mean()" and standard deviation "std()".  The variables with "Acc" are measurements from the gyroscope, and variables with "Gyro" are measurements from the accelerometer.  X, Y, and Z are directions.  The prefix 't' indicates time domain variables, and prefix 'f' indicates frequency domain variables.
